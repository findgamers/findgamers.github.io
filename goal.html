What We Know

Not much is known about what happened, but we know that the owner went missing before Gamer's closed (and maybe the reason why it is closed). He was acting very strange a few days before he went missing. Usually he would greet and welcome all his customers and staff workers, but those few days he would just hide in his office and never set foot outside until closing time.


What are our Goals?

The truth of what happened to Gamer's and its owner.
